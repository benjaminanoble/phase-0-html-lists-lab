Kelleye's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of breadp
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
